Hyperlinked Text

By Sijmen J. Mulder

This is a directory of website that primarily stick with simple, marked up,
hyperlinked text. I appreciate these sites because they load quickly, scroll
smoothly, spare my battery, are more compact, and lack the usual nonsense that
infects many websites.
